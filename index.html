Engineering

Warmth held inside a hairline frame.

Every LUMERA system is built around a continuous thermal break — a polyamide barrier that stops heat escaping through the frame. The result is comfort at the glass edge, no condensation, and slim sightlines that put the view first.

  • 01
    Thermal break up to 64 mm

    Passive-house-grade insulation across the profile.

  • 02
    Double & triple glazing

    Argon-filled units tuned for heat, glare and noise.

  • 03
    European hardware

    ROTO, HOPPE and SIEGENIA for a lifetime of smooth action.
