The atelier

We make the frame disappear.

LUMERA designs and engineers glazed systems for architects, builders and homeowners who want light without compromise — slim sightlines, deep comfort and finishes that last.

Philosophy

Light is the material.

A great window is one you stop noticing — the room is brighter, quieter and warmer, and the frame simply gets out of the way. We obsess over the millimetres that make that happen: the depth of a thermal break, the radius of a corner weld, the exact tone of a coating in evening light.

From a single replacement window to a full curtain-wall façade, every LUMERA system is drawn, specified and finished to the same standard.
